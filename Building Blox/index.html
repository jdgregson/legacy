Welcome to BuildingBlox!


BuildingBlox! Drag the colored boxes around the screen to make things. After you move a block, click the "*" button to make a new one. Click "*all*" to make another copy of all blocks. Click the pictures at the bottom to change the background. What can you build?

TIP: If you drag the blox slower, they move a lot easier.
